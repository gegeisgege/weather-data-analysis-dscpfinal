üîç Key Findings

  • Peak Pollution Hours: PM2.5 concentrations peak during morning (7-9 AM) and evening (6-8 PM) rush hours, indicating traffic as a major contributor
  • Temperature Correlation: Negative correlation between temperature and PM2.5 levels suggests better atmospheric dispersion at higher temperatures
  • Station Variability: Significant differences observed between urban and rural monitoring stations, with urban areas showing higher pollution
